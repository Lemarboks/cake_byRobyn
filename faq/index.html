We require a 50% non-refundable deposit to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is due 3 days before collection or delivery. We accept EFT and major payment apps.

We can accommodate certain dietary requirements including gluten-free and dairy-free options. Please mention any allergies in your order form so we can advise on the best options for you.

Deposits are non-refundable. If you need to reschedule, please do so at least 7 days before your order date and we'll do our best to accommodate a new date subject to availability.

Tasting sessions are available for wedding cake bookings. Please mention this in your order form and we'll arrange a time that works for you.
